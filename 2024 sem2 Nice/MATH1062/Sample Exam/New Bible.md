## Z-test
### H (hypothesis)
- Null hypothesis $H_0:$ $\mu = \mu_0$
- Alternate hypothesis $H_1:$
	- $\mu \neq \mu_0$
	- $\mu < \mu_0$
	- $\mu > \mu_0$
